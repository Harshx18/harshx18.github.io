Thermal Performance of Heat Exchanger with Staggered Triangular Winglet Strip Insert
Experimental investigation of strip inserts with staggered triangular winglets (SISTW) at 4 pitch ratios (0.66, 1, 1.5, 2) and 3 attack angles (30°, 45°, 60°). Measured Nusselt number, friction factor, and thermal enhancement across Reynolds number range. Research resulted in INCOTHERM 2025 presentation and Indian Patent Application IN202611037015 A1.

Heat Transfer Enhancement with Inline Triangular Winglets in Cylindrical Tube Strip Insert
Experimental analysis of heat transfer and fluid flow behavior in a cylindrical tube with inline triangular winglets. Evaluated Nusselt number, friction factor, and thermal enhancement factor (TEF) under varying configurations and Reynolds numbers to optimize winglet geometry for maximum heat transfer performance.

FEA-Based Design Optimization of Vacuum Vessel Flange for Spherical Tokamak
Structural optimization of a 450×560mm rectangular SS304L flange with four CF-63 ConFlat ports for the SSST (Small Scale Spherical Tokamak) at IPR. Performed coupled transient thermal and static structural FEA across 4 plate thicknesses (13, 16, 19, 22mm) and 3 welding sequences under welding thermal stress, bolt pretension, and atmospheric pressure loading. Optimum: 16mm flange with symmetric welding sequence — 0.0825mm deformation, 246 MPa von-Mises stress, safety factor ~2.0. Guide: Mr. Vishal Verma, Spherical Tokamak Division.

🔋
CFD Simulation of Lithium-Ion Battery Pack Thermal Management
Modeled thermal behavior of a Li-ion battery pack using ANSYS Fluent. Studied temperature distribution, identified hotspot locations, and evaluated cooling channel effectiveness to prevent thermal runaway — a critical concern for EV battery safety and range optimization.
ANSYS FluentCFDEVThermal Management
🔧
Design, Modelling & Structural Analysis of Wall Wheel Cart Caddy
CAD modelling of the wall wheel cart caddy in Fusion 360 followed by FEA structural analysis in ANSYS Mechanical. Developed the complete 3D design, applied realistic load cases, evaluated von Mises stress distribution, identified critical stress concentration regions, and proposed design geometry modifications to improve the structural safety factor.
